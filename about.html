Scott Freiman

Scott Freiman • scott@qwire.com • Github Name: sfreiman1

It’s not every day that you meet the CEO of a public company who is also a world-renowned Beatleologist. The creator of software that transformed two industries and the creator and presenter of nine acclaimed documentary films. A seasoned executive who holds multiple patents - as comfortable with investors as with sales and product teams – and a former Yale professor.

It's not every day that you meet Scott Freiman.

Scott Freiman is a technology and e-business savvy senior executive and entrepreneur with extensive expertise in strategy, marketing, technology, and operations. He may be the only person to have sold out Carnegie Hall and been a Finalist for Ernst and Young’s Entrepreneur of the Year.

Mr. Freiman was co-founder and CEO of Credit Management Solutions, Inc. (formerly NASDAQ:CMSS), a publicly traded financial software and services company and one of the earliest e-business successes. Mr. Freiman co-founded Credit Management Solutions in 1987 shortly after graduating from Yale University with a B.S. in Computer Science and Music.

After selling CMSI, Mr. Freiman became a full-time composer and producer. Mr. Freiman composed music for more than ten films, and also acted as sound editor and mixer on many films. Mr. Freiman also built Second Act Studio, a state-of-the-art music and video studio for composition, recording, and production.

In 2009, Mr. Freiman created Deconstructing the Beatles (beatleslectures.com), a series of multimedia presentations about the composition and production techniques of the Beatles. Mr. Freiman has presented his lectures to sold-out audiences at theaters nationwide and has spoken about the Beatles at colleges, universities, and corporations, such as Pixar, Google, and Facebook. In the Fall of 2012, Mr. Freiman taught a semester course on “The Beatles In The Studio” at Yale University. Mr. Freiman’s insights into the music of the Beatles can be found in the book All the Songs: The Story Behind Every Beatles Release published in October 2013 by Black Dog and Leventhal. Mr. Freiman is featured in eleven Deconstructing the Beatles films currently showing in theatres throughout North America and available on DVD and streaming.

In 2013, Mr. Freiman co-founded Qwire Inc. The company’s software is being used to streamline music tracking and rights management for television series, films and other media. A strong advocate for music education for children, Mr. Freiman has taught composing and music technology to children and adults and created over 30 video training courses for AskAudio.com.
